APPETIZERS
  • FALAFEL

    A secret recipe for the best authentic falafel, made with chickpeas, fresh herbs and spices!


  • HALVA

    A traditional Middle Eastern fudge-like confection made with tahini (sesame seed paste), sugar, spices and nuts.


  • SABICH

    Sabich or sabih is an Israeli sandwich of pita or laffa bread stuffed with fried eggplants, hard boiled eggs, Salat Katzutz, parsley, amba and tahini sauce.


  • MEZZE PLATTER

    A tasty platter adorned with hummus, tabbouleh, almonds, lavash bread, olives, feta cheese, cucumbers, tomatoes, and artichoke hearts.


  • CHICKEN SKEWERS

    Our farm raised chicken and fresh summer vegetables are marinated in a light Mediterranean-style marinade of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and spices, then grilled on skewers until golden brown.


  • SALMON SALAD

    Our salmon salad comes fully loaded with pan seared, tender-crisp salmon fillets, cucumber, olives, tomatoes, feta cheese and avocados drizzled in the best lemon herb mediterranean dressing!


SEAFOOD
  • PAELLA

    A seafood stew with a tomato base, our paella is slow cooked with marinated calamari and prawns, and lightly seasoned with sea-salt, black pepper, and sumac. We add croutons to give for added a crunchy taste.


  • CRISPY ZA'ATAR FISH

    Za'atar is a pre-made spice blend of aromatic dried thyme or oregano, tart sumac, and nutty sesame seeds. Here, it flavors fish fillets served over a warm salad of wilted chard, crisp pomegranate seeds, briny olives, and salty feta.


  • SHABBAT FISH

    Classic buttery, garlicky, and citrusy tilapia fillets served over our Israeli couscous.


  • SEARED SCALLOPS

    A light dish that deivers a rich peppery nutty flavor, served over chewy Israeli couscous.



  • HARISSA-SPICED SALMON

    A crisp-skinned salmon fillet, cooked with a spicy harissa and sweet agave, and paired with our tangy red grape house salad.



  • CHILI, LEMON, AND BASIL SHRIMP

    Spicy and hot with a zing of lemon, and added basil sweetness, and served with toasty pita bread and hummus.


SALADS
  • RICKY'S HOUSE SALAD

    A robust salday made with peppery arugula, hearty chickpeas, cool cucumbers, smoky roasted reds, tangy feta cheese and an herby vinaigrette.


  • PAREVE "ISRAELI SALAD"

    This chopped tomato and cucumber is sprinkled with pine nuts, herbs, and cheese. Pareve is a popular choice in Tel-Aviv


  • QUINOA SALAD

    Made with cucumbers, tomatoes, kalamata olives, red onion, extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon and Feta cheese, this filling salad is packed with tasy Mediterranean flavors.


  • MORTIMORE'S DELIGHT

    Bite into this salad filled with romaine, chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell peppers, roasted red peppers, feta, fresh herbs, lemon garlic shrimp, and a zesty Greek vinaigrette.

  • GRILLED CHICKEN SALAD

    This grilled chicken salad combines marinated chicken, fresh veggies, and lemon and olive dressing for bold tasty flavors.


  • ORANGE AND POMEGRANATE SALAD

    Made with a sprinkle of warm spices, and a bright lime dressing with a touch of honey and orange blossom water, this salad is a tasty mix of naval oranges pomegranate arils, and shallots.


MAIN COURSE
  • KATAIFI NESTS WITH MAURITANIAN GROUND LAMB

    Seasoned ground lamb and zucchini nestled perfectly inside our delicious kataifi.


  • BEEF MOUSSAKA WITH MATBUCHA

    Made with thick layers of eggplant, beef in tomato sauce, topped with béchamel sauce.


  • SHAWARMA

    A juicy chicken marinated in a blend of Middle Eastern spices. Enjoy this dish as a wrap with tomato, lettuce, and a simple yoghurt sauce on the side, or with rice and a salad.


  • ROASTED RACK OF LAMB

    Tender, oven roasted rack of lamb marinated in garlic and herbs.


  • SUMAC DUSTED BEEF SKEWERS WITH SPICY MANGO CHUTNEY

    A brilliant combination of the Middle Eastern Spice Sumac with the tangy sweetness of mango chutney on beef skewers.

  • TAHINI MEATBALLS

    These beefy meatballs in tahini are baked with roasted vegetables for added flavor, and are served with our Israeli salad.


DESERT
  • MALABI ROSE WATER MILK PUDDING

    Silky textured and light, the flower-scented pudding is topped with sweet syrup and nuts or shredded coconut.

  • HALVA

    Our halva cake is dense and moist; combining the familiar taste of Tel-Aviv with a hint of Turkish sweetness
